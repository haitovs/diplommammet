          Quick start

          How the round flows

          • Pick a difficulty plus an optional region to filter the atlas.
          • Read three layered clues, then lock in a country guess.
          • Need help? Tap Hint to reveal curated extra facts.
          • Use AI Boost to get scripted reasoning tips without leaving the page.

          What counts

          Scoring & streaks

          • Correct answers award more points on higher difficulties.
          • Submitting a player name adds your best run to the leaderboard.
          • Hints reduce the available pool of points for the round.
          • Region filters keep streaks thematic (Nordic, Horn of Africa, etc.).
